The Riddlekeeper's Library

Game Description:

A 2D fantasy puzzle point-and-click game I designed and developed about a trapped dragon friend that has promised you a legendary book. Your task is to solve riddles and move through the abandoned library to save your friend.

My Role:

For this project i was the only person contributing so i did the art and assets, the code, the ui, the sound i got off the internet and coded it in.
